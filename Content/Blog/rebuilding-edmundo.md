The site is moving from a placeholder into a real personal home base.

The goal is simple: keep a public place for notes, activities, trips, photos, videos, links, and small project records without turning the whole thing into a business brochure.

Business work can still point to [unwhelm.net](https://unwhelm.net), but edmundo.com can stay looser and more personal.

## What changed

- The site now runs as an ASP.NET Core Razor Pages app.
- The blog is its own reusable Razor Class Library.
- Posts live as Markdown files so they can be edited, backed up, and moved easily.
