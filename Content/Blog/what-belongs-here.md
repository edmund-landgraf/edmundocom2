A personal site does not need to perform like a product page.

It can be a shelf. Some things are polished, some are notes, and some are there because they are useful to keep close.

## Good candidates

- Activities and current interests.
- Trip notes and photo links.
- Project records and build notes.
- Public links that point to authenticated services when needed.
- Thoughts that deserve more than a fleeting post.
